A Lifetime of Healthy Vision

Vision should not be a barrier to learning, working, playing, or living independently.

Your donation today will fund outreach to people at the highest risk of vision loss. It will support quality vision screenings for preschool and school-aged children and provide access to professional eye care for low income families around the country. Your donation will fund advocacy and research toward the goal of helping more Americans enjoy the gift of sight for a lifetime.
